# TÜBİTAK 2209-A Undergraduate Research Project

## Project Title
A Campus-Based Awareness Study on Sustainable Water Use with University Students

## Project Overview
This TÜBİTAK 2209-A supported undergraduate research project focuses on increasing awareness of sustainable water use among university students through survey-based behavioral analysis and structured awareness activities conducted in a campus environment.

The project measures and improves water usage awareness through surveys, seminars, visual materials, and behavioral impact assessment.

## Objectives
- Analyze student water usage behaviors through structured surveys
- Identify water saving potential in campus environments
- Increase awareness of sustainable water consumption
- Encourage behavioral change through educational activities
- Measure impact using pre- and post-activity surveys
- Support Sustainable Development Goals related to water efficiency

## Methodology
The project was conducted in multiple stages:
- Initial survey for behavioral measurement
- Data analysis of usage patterns
- Awareness activities including seminars and campus events
- Visual information materials and campaigns
- Follow-up survey and comparative evaluation
